## ReelDesa — Design Guidelines

### Mood
Cinematic, warm, premium, grounded. Think golden-hour film grade over Indonesian village landscapes. Calm and spacious, not busy. A craft tool for a creator, not a corporate dashboard.

### Color palette
- **Background:** deep, warm near-black / charcoal (cinematic letterbox feel). e.g. `#14110E` base, slightly lighter `#1F1B16` panels.
- **Primary accent (golden / film-grade warmth):** `#E0A95C` → `#F2C57C` — used for CTAs, progress, key highlights, the "cinematic" upgrade signal.
- **Secondary accent (lush natural green):** `#3E6B4F` / `#5C9A6E` — supporting accents, success states, "delivered" confirmation.
- **Text:** warm off-white `#F4EFE6` primary, muted `#A89F90` secondary.
- Keep high contrast for readability on the dark base.

### Typography
- Headlines: a refined, slightly cinematic serif or strong geometric sans (e.g. Fraunces / Playfair for display warmth, or Inter tight for sans). Large, confident.
- Body/UI: clean humanist sans (Inter), generous line-height.
- Numbers/stats (resolution, duration): tabular, slightly mono feel for the before/after stats.

### Layout & components
- Generous whitespace, centered single-column focus for the upload→process→download flow — it's a single hero task, treat it like a stage.
- A clear 3-step progression (Intake → Enhance → Deliver) — stepper or staged reveal.
- Upload: large, inviting drop zone with subtle warm border glow.
- Processing: smooth golden progress indicator, calm not frantic.
- Before/After: tasteful comparison of source vs cinematic result with resolution stats.
- Buttons: golden primary fill for the main action; quiet ghost buttons for secondary.
- Soft elevation, gentle rounded corners (12–16px), subtle film-grain or vignette texture allowed but never noisy.

### Motion
Slow, smooth, confident transitions (300–500ms ease). The "enhance" moment should feel like a reveal — a satisfying golden bloom from raw to cinematic.

### Logo / brand
ReelDesa wordmark, golden on dark. Cinematic + warm + village.
