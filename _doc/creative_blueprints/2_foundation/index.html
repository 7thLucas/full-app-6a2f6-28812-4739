The Foundation

The bottleneck between raw and remarkable

The Problem

Authentic villager footage is what makes a tourism film feel alive — but it arrives low-res and heavily compressed from old phones. Cleaning and upscaling every clip by hand is slow, and it caps how many villages one editor can take on.

The Solution

One clean pipeline: villagers or the editor drop in rough clips, the app upscales them to cinematic grade, and the editor pulls timeline-ready footage straight into the edit — authenticity preserved, quality met.
